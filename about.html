Welcome to the medEasy !!!

“The miracle is this: The more we share the more we have.”

We are team of problem solvers, who are trying to contribute towards Healthy India mission. For which we have developed a platform wherein a user as a donar can donate medicines to needy and help them to recover. The platform for medicine donation acts as an interface between the users who are looking for a channel to give the extra medicines without wasting it. It enables us to donate the extra medicines by notifying nearby users with the details of the medicine that is available.

The focus of this project is to reduce the amount of medicine wasted and being used to the needy people. We gather medicine surpluses, medicine which otherwise would go to waste. Through medicine donations, we are able to help 5% of poverty-stricken households. Currently, we are only able to help those people who need it the most.

80 million people out of 1.2 billion Indians, roughly equal to 6.7% of India's population. Did you know that this dire situation can be solved simply by giving medicine surpluses? A solution both good for people and the environment. Every week our volunteers hand out medicine to those who need it most. Companies, institutions, governments and individuals offer their support through funds, medicines and services.
